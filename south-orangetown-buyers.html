Know Your Options

Not All Loans Are Created Equal

Conventional Loan
Strong Credit
Down payment: 5–20% · Credit score: 620+
Competitive rates, flexible terms. PMI applies under 20% down. Best for buyers with stable income and solid credit history.
FHA Loan
Most Popular
Down payment: as low as 3.5% · Credit score: 580+
Government-backed, flexible qualification standards. The most common choice for first-time buyers in Rockland County.
VA Loan
Veterans Only
Down payment: 0% · No PMI required
Exclusively for eligible veterans and active military. One of the best mortgage products available — if you qualify, use it.
USDA Loan
Rural Areas
Down payment: 0% · Income limits apply
For eligible rural and suburban areas. Worth checking if you're looking at properties outside the core of South Orangetown.
Money on the Table

Down Payment Assistance Programs You Should Know

SONYMA — Achieving the Dream New York State's lowest interest rate mortgage program. Just 3% down with down payment assistance available. Designed for first-time buyers with good credit and stable employment. Income limits apply.
SONYMA — DPAL (Down Payment Assistance Loan) A forgivable loan for your down payment — no monthly payments, zero interest, fully forgiven after 10 years as long as you stay in the home. Stacks with SONYMA mortgage programs.
Rockland County First-Time Buyer Programs County-level assistance, employer-based housing benefits, and local grants are available. Many buyers never explore these because they assume they don't qualify. Talk to me before you assume anything is out of reach.
$1,500 Closing Credits* Eligible buyers who work with a Real agent and finance through One Real Mortgage may receive up to $1,500 toward closing costs. A real, tangible reduction in what you bring to the table on closing day.

You Don't Need 20% Down. You Just Need the Right Plan.

The number one reason first-time buyers in South Orangetown sit on the sidelines is a myth. They believe they need 20% down to buy a home. That is simply not true. And it's costing people years of equity and appreciation they'll never get back.

South Orangetown sits in the heart of Rockland County — strong school districts, easy access to the Tappan Zee, commuter rail, and the kind of established neighborhoods where values hold. This is not a market to wait on. The buyers who figured out financing first are the ones who got in. The ones who waited are still waiting.

"Pre-approval strengthens your offer immediately in competitive South Orangetown neighborhoods."

Here's what I tell every first-time buyer I sit down with: your financing structure is more important than the home you find first. FHA loans can get you in with as little as 3.5% down. SONYMA's programs offer forgivable down payment assistance that effectively turns a loan into a grant if you stay for 10 years. And there are county-level and employer-based benefits most people never even ask about.

The other piece nobody talks about is the difference between pre-qualification and pre-approval. Pre-qualification is a conversation. Pre-approval is a commitment. In a market where good homes move fast, sellers in South Orangetown are looking at offers from pre-approved buyers first. If you haven't done that step, you're already behind.
