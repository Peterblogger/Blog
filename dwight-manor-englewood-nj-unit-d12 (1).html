They don't build them like this anymore. Dwight Manor has stood on Englewood's East Hill since 1927 — a landmark Tudor building with the kind of character, craftsmanship, and address that no new construction can replicate. Unit D12 is your chance to own a piece of it.

The Building

Dwight Manor is one of Englewood's most recognizable and beloved addresses — a grand pre-war Tudor complex set among mature trees on the highly desirable East Hill. The building features a private gated entrance, on-site fitness center, assigned parking, and laundry facilities. Heat and hot water are included. This is not a cookie-cutter condo complex — it's a landmark.

East Hill is Englewood's most sought-after pocket — tree-lined streets, walkable to vibrant downtown dining, boutiques, and cafes, and just steps from NJ Transit bus routes with direct service to the Port Authority in Midtown Manhattan. You're 20 miles from the city with a suburb's peace and a city-dweller's convenience.
